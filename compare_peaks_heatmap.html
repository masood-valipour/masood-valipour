Figure Overview and Methodology

These plots display the marginal distribution heatmaps with maximum peak positions overlaid for three different quantum states: Optimum, Entangled, and Unentangled.

  • Heatmap Background: Logarithmic color scale showing the probability density p(ω) computed from analytical formulas.
  • Peak Lines: Orange and cyan curves show the positions of local maxima in the distributions.
  • Calculation Method: Marginal distributions are computed using state-specific formulas, with peaks found by root-finding on the derivatives.
