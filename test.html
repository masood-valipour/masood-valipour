Overview

This project studies how the marginal distributions and peak locations change under different quantum states and detuning conditions. The analysis focuses on optimum, entangled, and unentangled two-photon states.

Heatmaps show the marginal probability distributions, while colored curves indicate the maximum peak positions.

Methodology

Peak Extraction

Peak positions are extracted from the marginal distributions by locating the roots of the derivative.

Heatmaps

Background colors represent probability density in the marginal frequency distributions.

Reference Frequencies

Dashed reference lines correspond to the \(\omega_{fe}\) and \(\omega_{eg}\) transition frequencies.

Interactive Controls

Sliders allow real-time exploration of detuning and decay-rate effects.
