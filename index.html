Optimization of Two-Photon Absorption for a Three-Level Atom

This project focuses on optimizing two-photon absorption processes in three-level systems by tailoring entangled and unentangled Gaussian photon pairs. The work combines analytical derivations and GPU-accelerated numerical simulations.

🔗 arXiv · Physical Review A · GitHub

CMB Dipole Asymmetry through Annular Variance

This work proposes a new method to estimate the CMB dipole direction and amplitude using annular variance comparisons. Applied to Planck data, it finds a dipole significance of 1.6σ, offering insights into possible isotropy violations.

🔗 Iranian Journal of Physics Research · Google Scholar
